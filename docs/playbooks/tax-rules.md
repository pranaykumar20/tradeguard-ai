# Tax & Lot Rules

Tax-aware trading guardrails for rebalancing and wash-sale avoidance.

## Wash sale window

Avoid repurchasing the same ticker within 30 days after a loss-generating sale if you intend to claim the loss for taxes. TradeGuard tracks open lots and flags wash-sale risk during rebalance proposals.

## Tax-lot awareness

When selling for a loss, prefer specific lot selection and confirm holding period. Short-term gains are taxed at ordinary income rates — factor this into CAUTION verdicts on quick flips.
