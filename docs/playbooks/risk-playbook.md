# Risk Playbook

Core guardrails for volatile markets and tech-heavy portfolios.

## QQQ and VIX regime

Never add to a tech-heavy portfolio when QQQ is below its 50-day moving average and VIX is rising. Reduce position size by 40% in high-volatility regimes.

## Single-name concentration

Single-name concentration above 20% requires explicit user approval. NVDA, META, MSFT combined with QQQ creates hidden correlation risk.

## Order types and timing

No market orders on volatile tickers. Use limit orders with defined stop loss. Do not trade in the first 10 minutes after market open.

## Daily loss circuit breaker

Daily loss circuit breaker: halt all new trades when daily P&L exceeds the configured loss limit. Review open positions before resuming.
